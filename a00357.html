Calculate the aerodynamic forces on the immersed body surface: for each "local facet", i.e., facets (of the immersed body surface) that lie within the local computational domain of this MPI rank, compute the surface pressure and the shear forces at the facet centroid from the flow variables at the grid points surrounding the facet.
